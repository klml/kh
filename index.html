kh by klml

small JS onscreen tools

kleiner Helferlein

aka little helper
  • backkey accorded keyboardlayout for only 6 keys (*sdf jkl*) more on umija.org/dev:backkey
  • bulkeditor bulk jobs you cant do with a normal texteditor like, sotiing, search and replace a lot of pairs of terms (*poor mans vlookup*)
  • textsnake draw lines and rectangle with keyboard arrows. use its for small line-charts like 
    ┌─┐              ┌──┐  ┌──┐    ┌──────┐    ┌──┬─────┐

│ │                 └──┘     │  ┌─────┘  └────┘  └──┘
└─┘                          └──┘
